1Overview

MK Collectible Cars LLC ("MK Collectible Cars," "we," "our," or "us") operates the website mkcollections.kaz-b.com and is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, contact us, submit a financing application, or otherwise interact with us. Please read it carefully. If you disagree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please discontinue use of our site.

In short: We collect information you provide to us directly (such as through our Apply Online form or contact messages), along with basic technical data about how you use our website. We use this information only to operate our business and communicate with you. We do not sell your personal data.

2Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

We collect information you voluntarily submit to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you contact us or submit an inquiry
  • Personal and financial information submitted through our financing application form (name, address, date of birth, employment status, income, and credit information)
  • Messages and correspondence you send us via our website chat or contact form
  • Trade-in vehicle details you choose to share with us

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, certain information is collected automatically by our hosting and analytics infrastructure:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location (city/region level)
  • Browser type and version, operating system, and device type
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URL
  • Date and time of each visit

This information does not identify you personally and is used only in aggregate form to understand how our website is being used.

3How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries — to contact you in response to a question, vehicle inquiry, or test drive request you submitted
  • To process financing applications — to evaluate and respond to credit applications submitted through our Apply Online form
  • To facilitate vehicle transactions — to assist in the purchase, sale, or consignment of vehicles
  • To improve our website — to understand how visitors interact with our site and make improvements
  • To comply with legal obligations — to meet applicable legal, regulatory, and compliance requirements
  • To prevent fraud — to detect and prevent fraudulent transactions or misuse of our services

We do not use your information for automated profiling or for any purpose beyond what is reasonably necessary to operate our dealership and serve you.

4Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

Service providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business (such as form processing services, email providers, and web hosting). These parties are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the specific services they provide to us.

Financing and lending partners

If you submit a financing application, we may share your information with lenders, banks, or financing companies for the purpose of evaluating your credit application. You will be informed before any such sharing takes place.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

Business transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change in ownership or control of your personal information.

5Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses a small number of cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function, such as saving your light/dark theme preference
  • Analytics — we may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns. No personally identifiable information is included in analytics data

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website functionality (such as theme preferences not being saved). We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

6Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Our website is served over HTTPS to encrypt data in transit.

Financing applications submitted through our website are processed by Formspree, a third-party form processing service with its own security and privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy policy at formspree.io.

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law (such as for tax, accounting, or other legal requirements).

General inquiries and contact messages are typically retained for up to 2 years. Financing application data may be retained longer as required by applicable financial regulations.

To request deletion of your data, please contact us using the information in Section 11.

8California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know — you have the right to request that we disclose what personal information we have collected about you, the sources of that information, and the purposes for which it is used
  • Right to Delete — you have the right to request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale — we do not sell personal information, so this right does not apply, but you may contact us to confirm this
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights

To submit a verifiable consumer request, please contact us at m22k.cars@gmail.com or call +1 747-441-6336. We will respond within 45 days of receiving a verifiable request.

9Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to individuals under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 16, please contact us immediately and we will delete that information promptly.

10Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
