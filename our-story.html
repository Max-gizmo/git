How it started

Built on a love for the right car.

MK Collectible Cars LLC was founded in Los Angeles with one simple idea: that buying a collectible or performance car should be a genuinely good experience. No pressure, no hidden history, no surprises after you drive home.

We started by focusing on what we know best — hand-built AMG engines, Porsche driving dynamics, BMW M purity, and the kind of collectible vehicles that hold value because they were built to be remembered. Every car in our inventory is personally selected, inspected, and backed by a clean title.

What we believe

Transparency isn't optional — it's the whole point.

We believe the car you buy should be exactly the car you were told you were buying. That means honest descriptions, complete service records when available, and real conversations about condition instead of polished sales pitches.

Every vehicle gets a full mechanical inspection before it goes on the lot. If there's something worth noting about a car — we'll tell you first. Our customers come back because they trusted us once and it worked out.

The specialty

Performance and collectible marques done right.

The used car market is full of vehicles. What's rare is a dealer who actually understands the difference between an M156-powered C 63 AMG and a regular C-Class, or why the Z4 M Coupe matters in a way that most two-seaters don't.

Our specialty is the cars that enthusiasts seek out — AMG V8s, Porsche flat-sixes and turbo sixes, BMW M straight-sixes. We know these cars well enough to find good ones, and we're honest enough to pass on the ones that aren't.

What we stand for

Our values.

Six principles that guide every car we acquire, every inspection we conduct, and every conversation we have with a buyer.

Clean Titles Only

Every vehicle we sell carries a clean title. No salvage, no flood, no rebuilt. We won't stock a car that we wouldn't personally recommend to a friend.

Honest Condition

We describe our cars as they actually are — not as we wish them to be. Every flaw worth noting is disclosed before, not after, you make a decision.

Full Inspection

Before any car joins our inventory, it goes through a comprehensive mechanical inspection. We know what we're selling — and you'll know too.

Enthusiast Knowledge

We specialize in AMG, Porsche, BMW M, and collectible vehicles. We know these platforms deeply — the good variants, the ones to avoid, and why it matters.

Real Support

Our relationship with buyers doesn't end at the sale. Questions about your car, sourcing your next one, or just an honest opinion — we're available by phone or in person.

Curated, Not Bulk

We're a boutique dealership, not a volume lot. Every car is personally selected. We'd rather have seven exceptional cars than seventy mediocre ones.

Our history

The journey.

2018

Founded in Los Angeles

MK Collectible Cars LLC opens at 1333 E Washington Blvd, Los Angeles with a focus on collectible and performance vehicles. First inventory includes AMG and BMW M models sourced from private collections.

2019

Expanding the Collection

Added Porsche to our core specialty brands. Introduced our consignment service, allowing collectors to sell their vehicles through MK Cars while we handle every detail.

2021

5-Star Recognition

Achieved 5-star ratings on both CarGurus and Kelley Blue Book, reflecting consistent buyer satisfaction. Launched our professional detailing service — paint correction, ceramic coatings, and interior care.

2023

Growing the Team

Expanded to include dedicated financing assistance to help more buyers access the collectible cars they want. Strengthened relationships with service partners across the LA area.

2025

Today

MK Collectible Cars continues to operate from our Los Angeles location, hand-selecting the best examples of AMG, Porsche, BMW M, and rare collectible vehicles. Every car still personally inspected, every title verified clean.
